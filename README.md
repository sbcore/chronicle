# Chronicle — A Simple Time Tracker
Chronicle is a single-page web app that helps you track how you spend your time during the day. Open it, type what you're working on, hit Start. That's the whole idea.
No sign-up. No installation. No subscription. Just a clean page that does its job.

---

## What It Does

You create tasks — things like *Office Work*, *SAP Prep*, or *Client Calls* — and track time against them throughout your day. You can run multiple tasks at the same time, pause when you take a break, and end when you're done. Chronicle quietly keeps a log of everything.

At the end of the day, or the end of the week, you can look back and see exactly where your hours went.

---

## Features

- **Multiple tasks at once** — pause one, start another, resume later
- **Continues across days** — your tasks carry over, so Monday's work continues on Tuesday
- **Daily and weekly summaries** — visual breakdown of time spent per task
- **Export your data** — download as PDF, Excel (.xlsx), or CSV
- **Feedback button** — send a message directly to the creator
- **Works on any device** — phone, tablet, or laptop

---

## How to Use It

1. Open the link in your browser
2. Enter your name — you'll only need to do this once
3. Type a task name and click **Add Task**
4. Hit **Start** when you begin working
5. Use **Pause** for breaks and **Resume** when you're back
6. Click **End** when you're done for the day

**Tip:** Bookmark the page on your phone or laptop so it's always one tap away.

**Example:** You sit down at 9am to answer emails. Type *Emails*, hit Start. At 9:45 you move to a report — hit Pause on Emails, type *Q2 Report*, hit Start. By Friday, you'll know exactly where your week went.

---

## Technical Notes

Chronicle is a single HTML file. There is no backend, no database, and no server-side code. Everything runs in your browser and your data stays on your device using localStorage.

To self-host it, download `index.html` and open it in any browser, or deploy it to any static hosting service like GitHub Pages, Netlify, or OneDrive.

**To enable the feedback form:** create a free account at [web3forms.com](https://web3forms.com), get your access key, and paste it into the `WEB3FORMS_KEY` line near the top of the file.

---

## Built With

- Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks or build tools
- [DM Sans & DM Mono](https://fonts.google.com) — typography
- [SheetJS](https://sheetjs.com) — Excel export
- [Web3Forms](https://web3forms.com) — feedback form delivery
